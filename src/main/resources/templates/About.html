About Travel Forum

Our Mission

Travel Forum is a vibrant community platform designed to connect travelers from around the world. Whether you're planning your next adventure, sharing memorable experiences, or seeking travel advice, our forum provides the perfect space to engage with fellow wanderers and explore the world together.

What We Offer

  • Share Your Stories: Create posts about your travel experiences, tips, and recommendations
  • Engage with Community: Comment on posts, like content, and connect with fellow travelers
  • Discover Destinations: Browse through a wealth of travel information and user-generated content
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-navigate design that makes sharing and discovering content a breeze

The Team

This forum was created by Ivo, Dimitar, and Nicol as their final project for the Telerik Academy Alpha Java Track. Built with passion and dedication, this platform showcases modern web development practices using Spring Boot, Thymeleaf, and a robust RESTful API.

Technology Stack

Our platform is built using cutting-edge technologies including:

  • Spring Boot for backend services
  • Thymeleaf for dynamic templating
  • MariaDB for data persistence
  • RESTful API architecture
  • Responsive web design
